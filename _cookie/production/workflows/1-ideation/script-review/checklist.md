# Script Review Checklist

## Required Checks
- [ ] Canonical script loaded and confirmed in correct format
- [ ] Brand guidelines loaded and referenced
- [ ] Narrative coherence reviewed and scored (1-5)
- [ ] Brand alignment reviewed and scored (1-5)
- [ ] Pacing and hook effectiveness assessed
- [ ] Technical feasibility of all directions evaluated
- [ ] Cost implications estimated and compared to budget
- [ ] Review decision recorded (approved or revise)

## Quality Standards
- [ ] Hook is compelling within first 3 seconds (short-form) or 10 seconds (long-form)
- [ ] Logical narrative flow from introduction through conclusion
- [ ] Transitions between scenes are smooth and natural
- [ ] Tone matches brand voice guidelines
- [ ] Visual directions consistent with brand identity
- [ ] Content appropriate for target audience
- [ ] No technically impossible or extremely risky directions
- [ ] Estimated cost within budget thresholds
- [ ] Pacing maintains engagement throughout

## Output Verification
- [ ] Complete review document produced with all assessments
- [ ] Overall recommendation clearly stated (approve/revise)
- [ ] Specific revision suggestions provided if not approved
- [ ] Revision suggestions prioritized by impact
- [ ] Decision record includes reviewer identity and timestamp
- [ ] Any approval conditions clearly documented
